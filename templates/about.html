About Smart Bangladesh

Our Mission

Smart Bangladesh is a digital platform designed to provide seamless access to government services for citizens. We aim to modernize bureaucratic processes and make public services more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

Our Vision

To create a digital Bangladesh where citizens can access government services with ease, transparency, and efficiency from anywhere, anytime.

Services Offered

  • Birth Certificate Registration
  • Death Certificate Registration
  • Family Certificate Services
  • Police Clearance Certificates
  • Healthcare Appointments
  • Trade License Applications
  • Blood Donation Services
  • Building Permits
  • Complaint Management
